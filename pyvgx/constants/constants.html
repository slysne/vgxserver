Constants

Several pyvgx constants are defined to control data types and the behavior of queries.

  • The D_ constants: Arc Direction Constants

  • The M_ constants: Arc Modifier Constants

  • The V_ constants: Value Condition Constants

  • The C_ constants: Collect Constants

  • The F_ constants: Result Field Constants

  • The R_ constants: Result List Entry Constants

  • The S_ constants: Sort Specification Constants

  • The T_ constants: Timestamp Constants

  • The OP_ constants: Opcode Constants
